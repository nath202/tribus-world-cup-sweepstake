How it updates

No setup needed

Scores, fixtures, stage progress and red cards sync automatically from ESPN's live World Cup feed. The page refreshes on its own every couple of minutes — or hit Sync in the header any time. Standings, goals, knockouts and reds are all worked out for you.

The Teams tab shows the detail and lets you nudge anything by hand if needed (handy for the odd edge case between syncs).

Everything lives in this browser (localStorage). Export a backup, or reset to reload the draw and clear results.
