How it updates

No setup needed

Scores, fixtures, stage progress and red cards sync automatically from ESPN's live World Cup feed. The page refreshes on its own every couple of minutes — or hit Sync in the header any time. Standings, goals, knockouts and reds are all worked out for you.

The Teams tab adapts to where the tournament is: full group tables during the group stage, then a "still in vs eliminated" view once the knockouts begin.
